Dermatologist

Precautions:

  • Use sunscreen daily

  • Avoid excessive sun exposure

  • Keep skin clean and moisturized

Spot Treatments:

  • Apply cold compress for minor burns

  • Use over-the-counter hydrocortisone for mild rashes

  • Apply aloe vera gel for sunburns

Cardiologist

Precautions:

  • Maintain a healthy diet low in saturated fats

  • Exercise regularly

  • Monitor blood pressure and cholesterol levels

Spot Treatments:

  • Administer aspirin for suspected heart attack

  • Perform CPR if necessary

  • Use nitroglycerin for angina (if prescribed)

Neurologist

Precautions:

  • Get adequate sleep

  • Manage stress levels

  • Avoid excessive alcohol consumption

Spot Treatments:

  • Rest in a quiet, dark room for migraines

  • Apply cold packs for headaches

  • Use FAST method to identify potential strokes

Orthopedic

Precautions:

  • Maintain proper posture

  • Use ergonomic equipment at work

  • Wear supportive footwear

Spot Treatments:

  • Apply RICE (Rest, Ice, Compression, Elevation) for sprains

  • Use heat therapy for chronic muscle pain

  • Immobilize suspected fractures
